Privacy Policy — RehearsAI

Last updated: March 2026

1. Data We Collect

Voice recordings (stored locally and optionally uploaded to Firebase), display names, device identifiers, and script text sent to Google Gemini AI for analysis.

2. How We Use Data

Script text is sent to Google Gemini API for dialogue extraction (retained 55 days, not used for AI training). Shared project data and voice recordings are stored on Google Firebase when you choose to share a project.

3. Data Storage

Local data stays on your device until you delete it. Firebase data is stored on Google servers (US). Gemini API retains prompts for 55 days for abuse monitoring.

4. Your Rights

You can delete all your data anytime from Settings → Delete All My Data. This removes local data and your data from our servers.

5. Third-Party Services

Google Gemini AI (script analysis), Google Firebase (data sharing), Google Play (payments).
