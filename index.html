Features

Everything your theater group needs

From script to stage — RehearsAI handles the tedious work so you can focus on the performance.

🤖

AI Script Extraction

Upload a PDF, Word, or text file. Enter character names and AI extracts all dialogues automatically — in any language.

👥

Team Collaboration

Share your project with a simple room code. Each actor selects their role. The director manages scenes and controls access.

Voice Recording

Record your lines and share them with the team via cloud. Download other actors' recordings to rehearse with real voices.

Text-to-Speech

Assign unique AI voices with adjustable pitch to each character. Rehearse the full scene solo with different voices.

Scene Management
