Features

Everything your theater group needs

From script to stage — RehearsAI handles the tedious work so you can focus on the performance.

🤖

AI Script Extraction

Upload a PDF, Word, or text file. Enter character names and AI extracts all dialogues automatically — in any language. Works in seconds, not hours.

👥

Team Collaboration

Share your project with a simple room code. Each actor selects their role. The director manages scenes, edits, and controls who can join.

🎤

Voice Recording

Record your lines and share them with the team via cloud. Download other actors' recordings to rehearse with real voices — compressed for minimal data usage.

🗣️

Text-to-Speech

Assign unique AI voices with adjustable pitch to each character. Rehearse the full scene solo with different voices — perfect for learning your cues.

🎬

Scene Management

Organize your script by scenes. Jump to any scene during rehearsal. See previous and next lines for context while practicing.

🌍

Multiple Languages

Full interface in English, German, French, Spanish, Italian, Turkish, and Arabic. Works with scripts in any language.
